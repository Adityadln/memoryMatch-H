Catch the Match

Instructions

Objective

Match cards of similar images in minimum possible flips before the time runs out. Timer is adjusted according to the difficulty selected and no of cards chosen(only for Hacker).

Hacker Mode

  • The player can dynamically select the number of cards.
  • Match cards having same animal emoji.
  • There will be two special cards present,Freeze and Bonus,only activated on choosing 24 cards or more.
  • As the name suggests,Freeze will freeze the timer for 5 secs while the bonus gives 2 extra points on selection.
  • Leaderboard is only activated on choosing exactly 36 cards.
  • On choosing more than 64 cards,there will be presence of Dummy cards in the card deck.

Normal Mode

  • There will be a total of 16 cards.
  • Match cards having same binary number
    (1 to 7)
  • Least number of flips will get you into leaderboard!!
